1 · The damage math

Damage = Base
       × (1 + Σ all "increased" %)        ← ONE additive bucket
       × (1 + additional₁) × (1 + additional₂) × …   ← each its own multiplier
       × enemy-side "takes additional" debuffs        ← more separate buckets

CONFIRMED Three rules govern everything:

  • All "increased" pools into one additive bucket. On a sheet already at 400% increased, +30% more ≈ ×1.06 — additive dilution.
  • Every distinct "additional" entry is its own multiplier. ×1.27 × 1.27 = ×1.61. Same-named additional affixes add within one entry — two "+20% additional lightning" rolls = ×1.4, not ×1.44.
  • Enemy-side debuffs are extra buckets: curses, Affliction (cap 100 = ×2.0 on DoT), Frostbite (cap 120), penetration, and now Crimson Tide +35-75% additional damage taken OFFICIAL.

Why this is the whole strategy: ten distinct ×1.3 buckets = ×13.8. The same investment as +300% increased on an endgame sheet ≈ ×1.6. Hunt new buckets, not bigger numbers in old ones.

Hit vs DoT — decisive for Terra. CONFIRMED DoT can't crit or double-damage; it scales via Affliction + Reaping. The new Terra actives are mixed: Thunderstorm/Tidal/Meteor tick hits (crit lives); Thornfield and Nether Grasp are persistent DoT. Allocate crit only to hit-based zones. Cast speed doesn't tick zones faster without explicit opt-in text. New SS13 zones last 6s base; old Terras were buffed 3s→6s.

2 · Ranked levers

#1 Dance of the Deep trait tree largest single lever OFFICIAL

  • Base kit: +5-20% additional Terra damage from trait level 2 (Jul 15 addendum removed the L1 grant and the +20% area); core Terra follows you; non-core Terra can't deal damage; +26% additional per Ground Charge consumed (printed on each skill).
  • Dreambreaker's Gyre → Crimson Tide: roaming entity in your zone; affected enemies take +35/45/55/65/75% additional damage — the single biggest enemy-side bucket in the kit.
  • The Paradise I Curse: auto lvl-1 Ominous curse in zone, +10-30% additional vs cursed. The Cycle Resisted: 25% of crit rating changes also scale core-Terra duration; recast = Reap 6-10% of remaining duration.
  • Layered Hem: +50-150% charge recovery; Spell Burst cap increases mirror onto charge cap (+2/2/3/3/3). Lonely Group Dance: Red Shadow phantoms recast your core skill (cap 1-3, -60→-40% dmg). Deep Crimson Endless Dance: random stances incl. Eternal Sleep (+66% dmg taken, 2s).
  • Catalyst: Terra nodes: auto-cast at full charges (+30% additional), mana- or life-seal variants. Cruel Red Dancing Shoes (moon affix): enemies take increased dmg = 38% of your movement-speed bonus (cap 75%) at cost of self true-damage — movement speed becomes a damage stat.

#2 +Active skill levels ~2.2-2.7× CONFIRMED

  • Steep base growth to lvl 20, then +10%/lvl multiplicative (21-30), +8%/lvl (31+); +8-12 levels gearable (necklace/gloves/shield/chest/legendaries).
  • Flame Tyrant's Steps rework: +1 Terra skill level per 20% fire res (max 3-5) OFFICIAL.

#3 Five support slots ~2-3× OFFICIAL

  • Real SS13 Terra supports center on Spell Burst stack consumption: Thunderclap (+167% to the bolt per stack consumed, +32-36% addl), Torrent hit-payoff/Crush (+100% per stack), Annihilation (+191-220% wave at 50 charges), Conduction (+5 current targets, +6.5-7%/enemy), Sacrifice (+2%/stack multiplicative), Retribution (+24-26% ×3 stacks for 10% life/s), Extermination (center +20% taken), Undertow (+1 torrent per 2 charges, -6→-3%).
  • Max Spell Burst stacks now double-dips: burst payoffs + charge cap via Layered Hem — a new cross-system scaling stat.
  • Energy 500+ to fill all slots (Bmlegolas); empty slot = missing multiplier layer.

#4 Legendary uniques ~1.5-2× OFFICIAL

  • Real S13 Terra items: Crimson Severance rework (per charge consumed +8% dmg ×8 stacks, core skill gains 25-level Terra support branch); King Soul Armor (+240-300% charge recovery); Flame Tyrant's Steps (+Terra levels via fire res). Staff/wand exalted suffix: +26-35% ground damage deepening — a brand-new bucket.
  • Generic additional-bucket items (mana-stacking +105%, Insight +30%) still apply.
  • Note: earlier leak items "Silent Monologue/Below the Styx as Terra gear" were wrong — official versions are dodge/channel items.

#5 Enemy-side damage-taken now the star OFFICIAL

  • Crimson Tide +35-75% additional taken + Eternal Sleep +66% taken (stance) + The Paradise I Curse auto-curse + Extermination center +20% — Dance of the Deep is unusually rich in enemy-side buckets; they multiply with everything player-side.
  • Classic menu still applies: Affliction ×2.0 on DoT components, Frostbite (cold), res shred/pen.

#6 Slates / pactspirit / prisms / memories ~1.1-1.4× each

  • Nether King slates OFFICIAL: Judgment (connection-line nodes +70%), Corruption (branded talents projected at 20%), Exile (random stances incl. sleep +100% taken). Prairie copy ≈ +35% addl (Bmlegolas).
  • Icemirror pactspirit OFFICIAL: +24/12% Terra dmg smalls, charge recovery + range; Stage in the Mirror: +4% Terra dmg/stack (→+8% at L5), ×3 stacks, L6 +1 charge cap at full stacks.
  • Haze Prism ≈ +12% addl +3 skill pts; movement-speed prism (+1% addl per +5% MS) — extra on-theme with Dancing Shoes.

#7 Crafted gear + uptime base layer

  • Weapon first (T2→T1→Priceless); increased% dilutes past ~300-500% total — prefer new buckets. Dream affixes removed in SS13 OFFICIAL.
  • Charge-economy affixes: staff/wand suffix +113-150% charge recovery; scepter/helmet exalted +stacks with +5-8% addl.
  • Movement speed is now triple-duty: Dancing Shoes damage-taken amp, Deep Sea uptime, prism addl.

Anti-patterns

  • Crit/double-damage on persistent components (Thorn, Abyssal) — hit-based zones only.
  • Same-bucket stacking: increased% dilution; same-named additional affixes add, not multiply.
  • Non-core Terra skills deal no damage under Dance of the Deep — one core skill, build around it.
  • Ignoring energy/uptime — both zero out whole buckets.
