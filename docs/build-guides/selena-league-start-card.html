Trait order

  1. Dance of the Deep base — zone follows you; +5-20% additional Terra dmg from L2 (Jul 15 nerf)
  2. L45: The Paradise I Curse — auto-Ominous + 10-30% additional vs cursed (Don's order, post-nerf)
  3. L60: The Cycle I Resist — generic crit chance scales Terra duration; recast-Reaps become the late-game carry (scale w/ Reap Duration + crit chance)
  4. L75: Dreambreaker's Gyre — Crimson Tide +35-75% additional taken
  5. Layered Hem / Silencing Severance (auto-cast) per slot availability

Loop: bank Terra Charges → cast consumes all → +26% additional per charge + Thunderclap +167%/Spell-Burst stack on the Thunderstrike. Base charge economy (tlidb beta): cap 1, gain 1 per 0.5s — cap raises via Layered Hem mirror, Silencing Severance, gear.

Alternative route (Allie's starter): Nether Grasp DoT — Cycle I Resist Reaps (crit rating double-dips: crit + duration→Reap), Gyre early → Paradise + Cycle + crit at 75, Psychic/Blur defense (Chain behind the Curtain · Eternal Sea Fog · Folklore of Locking Fingers · Valerie's Night Stroll), STR-stack Fallen Star pivot post-PT8. Automation claimed from L45.

Item affixes for damage — buy/craft priority

  1. S — "+X% additional Lightning / Spell / Terra Skill Damage" — the word additional is everything; differently-worded affixes each multiply
  2. S — +Skill Levels: "+X Lightning/Spell Skill Level" (necklace +3-4 · gloves +2-4 · shield +5 · corrosion chest +2). Each level past 20 = +10% multiplicative
  3. S — Terra Charge stack cap +X · Terra Charge Restoration Speed (staff/wand suffix to +150%)
  4. A — Terra Damage Enhancement (staff/wand exalted suffix +26-35% — new S13 bucket) · Elemental/Lightning Res Penetration
  5. A — Max Spell Burst stacks — double-dips: Thunderclap payoff and Layered Hem charge-cap mirror
  6. A — Crit rating → 100% chance, then crit damage (Storm Field is hit-based; crit fully works)
  7. B — Movement Speed — Red Shoes of Cruelty converts 38% of MS bonus into enemy damage-taken (cap 75%): MS is a damage stat
  8. B — Flat lightning-to-spells · increased% Spell/Lightning (strong early, dilutes past ~300-400% total)
  9. B — Energy on gear → 500+ total — an empty support slot is a missing multiplier
  10. B — INT (+0.5% additional damage per point — stacker angle) · generic Crit Chance (scales Cycle's duration + Reaps) · scepter "Reaps 0.13-0.18s of DoT" affix (DoT route)

New S13 legendaries: Crimson Severance (necklace — Gash stacks per charge) · King Soul Armor (+240-300% charge restoration) · Flame Tyrant's Steps (+Terra levels via fire res) · The Three Saints (element-level chest)

Existing legendaries (Spell Burst triple-dip): Law of Greed (trinket — +30-40% dmg per Max Spell Burst, to +320%; Max Burst also feeds Thunderclap + Layered Hem) · Genius Battlegear (chest — +40% spell crit rating per burst charge consumed ×10) · Shadow of Thunderlight's Fury/Eyes (1H pair — spell/crit/Lucky + lightning skill level) · Thunder Jawbone (helmet — +15-20% additional Lightning) · Brainwave Emitter (+1 Max Burst) · Air Treaders (+80% MS on your Terra → Red Shoes fuel). Trap: Cold Sanctuary Prosthetics — non-core Terra deals no damage under this trait

Defenses

  • Elemental resistances to 60% cap first — non-negotiable
  • 4,000+ Life/ES combined for Traveler 7; scale from there
  • Life% / ES% on every piece + a regen/leech layer
  • S13 systems: Deflection 6%/layer (cap 4-10) · Evasion capped 75%
  • Self-damage watch: Red Shoes ticks true damage (8% of MS bonus/s, cap 15%); Thornfield/Retribution burn 10-20% max Life/s — regen must cover anything you take. Storm Field itself has no self-cost
